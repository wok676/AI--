## 3. 完整描述(Full description,上限 4000 字符)

### English

```
Snap your meal. Know your calories.

Calorie Tracker turns meal logging into a three-step habit: take a photo, confirm the result, save. Instead of searching a food database and typing portions by hand, you point your camera at your plate and let AI estimate the calories and macronutrients for you.

WHY PEOPLE USE CALORIE TRACKER
- Photo-first logging: snap a photo, pick one from your gallery, or just describe your meal in words ("a bowl of beef noodles and a fried egg"). AI reads it and returns an estimate in seconds.
- You stay in control: every result is fully editable. Rename a food, adjust the portion, remove an item, or add one manually before saving. The AI suggests; you decide.
- See your day at a glance: a daily progress ring shows calories consumed against your goal, with protein, carbs, and fat broken out.
- Build a gentle habit: an optional streak gently celebrates consistent logging — no guilt, no aggressive nudges.
- Track your trend: review past days and a 7-day calorie and macro trend to understand your eating patterns over time.

DESIGNED FOR A GLOBAL AUDIENCE
Calorie Tracker speaks 12 languages out of the box — English, Chinese, Hindi, Spanish, French, Arabic, Bengali, Portuguese, Russian, Urdu, Japanese, and Korean — with full right-to-left layout support for Arabic and Urdu. Because recognition is powered by AI vision rather than a fixed food list, it works with a wide range of dishes and cuisines.

PRIVACY YOU CAN UNDERSTAND
- Your food photos are used only for instant analysis. We do not keep original images long-term — we store only the recognized result data (foods, portions, calories, macros).
- Data is sent over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
- Permissions are requested only when you use the related feature: the camera only when you take a photo, photos only when you pick one.
- Delete your account anytime from Settings. Deletion is permanent and irreversibly removes your meal logs, goals, and profile from our active systems. A web-based deletion channel is also available.

A NOTE ON ACCURACY
Calorie and nutrition figures are AI estimates and may be inaccurate. They are for general informational purposes only and are not medical, dietary, or health advice. Always verify important values and consult a qualified professional for medical or nutritional decisions.

WHAT'S INCLUDED IN THIS VERSION
- Email sign-up and login, plus Sign in with Apple
- Photo, gallery, and text-based meal logging
- Editable AI results with calories and macros
- Meal grouping (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack)
- Daily summary with a calorie progress ring
- Daily history and a 7-day trend view
- Manual calorie goal, with an optional estimate helper
- 12 languages with RTL support

Calorie Tracker is free to use. No ads, no subscription, no in-app purchases.

Have feedback or a question about your data? Reach us through the contact details in the app's About page.
```

### 中文

```
拍下这餐,了解热量。

卡路里记录把记账式的饮食记录,变成「拍照 — 确认 — 保存」三步就能完成的习惯。不必再翻食物库、手动填份量,只要把镜头对准餐盘,AI 就帮你估算这一餐的热量与三大宏量营养素。

为什么选择卡路里记录
- 拍照即录:拍一张照片、从相册选一张,或者直接用文字描述(「一碗牛肉面加一个煎蛋」),AI 几秒内给出估算结果。
- 你说了算:每一项结果都可编辑。保存前可改食物名、调份量、删除或手动添加条目。AI 负责建议,决定权在你。
- 一眼看懂今天:首页的每日进度环显示已摄入热量与目标的对比,并分别列出蛋白质、碳水和脂肪。
- 温和地养成习惯:可选的连续记录天数为坚持记录加一点正向鼓励——不制造焦虑,不强行打扰。
- 看见趋势:回顾过去的每一天,并通过近 7 天的热量与宏量趋势了解自己的饮食规律。

为全球用户打造
卡路里记录内置 12 种语言——英语、中文、印地语、西班牙语、法语、阿拉伯语、孟加拉语、葡萄牙语、俄语、乌尔都语、日语和韩语——并为阿拉伯语和乌尔都语提供完整的从右向左布局。识别基于 AI 视觉而非固定食物库,因此能适配多种菜系与餐食。

看得懂的隐私说明
- 你的食物照片仅用于即时识别。我们不会长期保存原图,只保存识别出的结果数据(食物、份量、热量、宏量)。
- 数据通过加密(HTTPS)连接传输。
- 权限仅在你使用相关功能时申请:拍照时才用相机,选图时才访问相册。
- 你可随时在「设置」中注销账号。注销不可逆,将从我们的在用系统中永久删除你的饮食记录、目标与个人资料,同时提供网页注销通道。

关于准确性
热量与营养数值为 AI 估算,可能存在误差,仅供一般信息参考,不构成医疗、膳食或健康建议。重要数值请自行核对;涉及医疗或营养决策,请咨询专业人士。

本版本包含
- 邮箱注册登录,以及通过 Apple 登录
- 拍照、相册、文字三种记录方式
- 可编辑的 AI 识别结果(含热量与宏量)
- 按餐次归类(早餐、午餐、晚餐、加餐)
- 含热量进度环的每日汇总
- 每日历史与近 7 天趋势视图
- 手动设定热量目标,并提供可选的估算助手
- 12 种语言,支持 RTL

卡路里记录免费使用,无广告、无订阅、无内购。

对协议或你的数据有疑问?可通过应用内「关于」页的联系方式联系我们。
